QUT Anime and Manga

Now in T E C H N I C O L O U R (and Subtitles)!

Forget everything you heard or thought you knew about watching Anime and reading Manga because here at the QUT Anime and Manga Society (QAAM), we’re all about breaching barriers to connect students through their shared love of one of the best things Japan has to offer! (besides Nintendo and Waifu pillows :D) We are a very active specialist interest club with our WEEKLY ANIME AND MANGA SCREENINGS AND READINGS where we get together and WEEB out to our hearts content without any judgement whatsoever! We also run specialised themed events throughout the year and the level of genuine wholesome fun is over 9000! And the best thing? Whether you’re a seasoned otaku or new to anime, absolutely EVERYONE IS WELCOME!!! Don’t forget to LIKE OUR PAGE at https://www.facebook.com/groups/QUT.Anime.and.Manga and NOTICEBOARD at https://www.facebook.com/qutanimemanga to be kept up to date of our events!
